# Advanced Employee Manager for Farming Simulator 25

Welcome to the Advanced Employee Manager, a mod designed to bring a new level of depth and strategy to your farm management in Farming Simulator 25.

## Overview

This mod aims to overhaul the employee management system, moving beyond the simple "hire-and-fire" mechanic of the base game. My goal is to provide a more immersive experience where your employees are valuable assets, with unique skills and traits that grow over time. Proper management will be key to optimizing your farm's efficiency and cultivating a skilled workforce.

## Key Features (Under Development)

*   **Employee Skills & Progression:** Hire green hands and train them into seasoned professionals. Employees will gain experience and improve their skills in areas such as driving, harvesting, and equipment maintenance.
*   **Task Management:** Assign specific tasks and vehicles to your employees, creating a structured and efficient workflow for your daily operations.
*   **Detailed Roster:** Access a comprehensive menu to view your entire workforce, including their current status, skills, and assigned equipment.
*   **Economic Impact:** Employees will have wages based on their skill level. A well-managed team will be a worthy investment, while poor management could impact your bottom line.

## Development Status
